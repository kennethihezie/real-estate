My article wasn't directed at your company, but at all those who provide useless information to agents and call that information “leads.”

The fact is that we have cheapened the meaning of so many things in this society today that no one should be surprised that “leads” are included. To me, a lead is a customer-initiated contact where the contact enters their name, phone and/or email, and hopefully a relevant comment about what they are truly seeking. There's one more factor: the contact must have come from the client's site, not from an amalgamation site.

When a real prospect asks an agent a specific question about something seen on that agent's site, we call that the opening of a dialogue, and a real lead.

Everyone needs real leads.

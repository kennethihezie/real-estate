HouseValues

Capture prospective home sellers early—when they’re motivated & searching for a home valuation. Potential sellers visit HouseValues.com to request a free personalized home valuation. Their information—including property details and listing time frame—is then passed straight to you!

Experience HouseValues leads:

  • Own your ZIP code, exclusively
  • Get seller lead insights including property details and listing timeframe
  • Receive a guaranteed number of leads per month
  • Leads are sent directly to the CRM for easy follow up
  • Send a personalized home valuation with Market Leader’s built in CMA tool

Leads Direct

Be the local expert. With Leads Direct, our pay-per-click advertising campaigns drive consumers who are searching for home listings on Google, Bing, and other search engines directly to your personally branded IDX website.

Ensure a steady stream of leads with Leads Direct:

  • Capture home buyer and seller leads on your IDX website
  • Get lead insights including properties viewed
  • Only pay for leads, not visitors
  • Seamlessly integrated with Market Leader systems—all lead information, including home search criteria, is sent directly to your CRM

Market Leader’s real estate leads are truly exclusive. Every lead we generate on your behalf will be sent to one and only one agent—you! Other companies send leads to multiple agents at the same time, so you must race to contact leads first. This is inconvenient and stressful for agents and irritating for consumers.
